ENTERTAINMENT FACTORY

18 tiny apps. one new one every day.

Personal Emblem Forge

Enter your name and pick 3 personality traits (e.g., 'ambitious', 'curious', 'loyal'). The app procedurally generates a unique heraldic-style personal emblem on canvas — a shield shape, symbolic animal (seeded from name hash), pattern fills, accent colors, and a generated motto. Same inputs always produce the same emblem. Rendered in a bold medieval-woodcut aesthetic (black lines, limited rich palette). A 'Download my emblem' button exports the canvas as a shareable image. Entirely client-side, works on mobile.

Vibe Tax Calculator

Enter a vibe as plain text (e.g. 'Coachella influencer', 'dark academia TA', 'VC at a hackathon'). The app computes and returns an itemized 'Vibe Tax' receipt: line items like 'espresso martinis — $47', 'performative book — $22', 'lingering vape cloud — $3.50', totaling a surprising dollar figure plus a sassy auditor verdict. Deterministic on input. Presented as a crumpled thermal-printer receipt graphic — instantly screenshot-able.

Ransom Note Forge

A text-to-ransom-note generator. Type any short message and get it back as a classic cut-out-letters ransom note — each character rendered as a different magazine/newspaper clipping with varied fonts, paper textures, and rotations. Re-roll to reshuffle, and save the result as a PNG.

Texting Decoder

Reply to six rapid-fire texts the way you actually would (hey wyd, did u eat, cancel plans without being rude…). The app analyzes punctuation, capitalization, emoji use, abbreviations, and reply length to deterministically assign a named texting archetype — 'The Lowercase Romantic', 'Captain Capslock', 'The Ellipsis Poet', 'The "lol" Linguist', etc. The result shows your actual replies quoted as iMessage-style bubbles with annotated tells, ready to screenshot. Seeded: same 6 replies always yield the same archetype. Pastel-zine aesthetic with Archivo Black display type.

Slime Mold Simulator

Physarum polycephalum simulation where users place food sources on a dark canvas and watch a living network of slime mold tendrils grow, branch, and optimize paths between food. Users can add, remove, or drag food sources to reshape the network in real time. Visually mesmerizing — the network self-organizes into minimal spanning trees. A 'Save my organism' button exports the canvas as a shareable image.

Rhythm Tap

A canvas-based rhythm game where colored note tiles fall from the top of the screen in sync with a procedurally generated beat (Web Audio API). Tap or click each tile as it hits the target zone — perfect hits score more, misses break your combo. Songs increase in BPM as the score grows. At game over, players see their accuracy percentage, longest combo, and a grade (S/A/B/C).

Name to Galaxy

Type any name (or word) and watch the browser instantly render a unique procedural galaxy for it: star density, nebula color clouds, and a central object (star cluster or black hole silhouette) are all seeded from the name's character hash. The galaxy is named 'The [Name] Nebula' and a one-line flavor fact is pulled from NumbersAPI using the character count as the number seed. A 'Download my galaxy' button exports the full-resolution canvas as a shareable image. Entirely client-side, works on mobile.

Particle Life

A Particle Life simulation in the browser: hundreds of coloured particles follow simple attraction/repulsion rules that produce emergent flocking, clustering, and cell-like behaviour. Users can tweak the force matrix sliders in real time and watch the world reorganise. One 'Screenshot my universe' button exports a named snapshot (seeded from the current rule set) for sharing.

Crowd Simulator

Draw walls, place exits, release the crowd. Can you engineer a perfect escape?

Pipe Connect

A daily spatial puzzle: a grid of cells with colored endpoints — draw paths to connect matching pairs without crossing. One puzzle per day, freshly generated by date seed. After solving, share your completion time and a compact emoji-grid of your solution path as a spoiler-free result. Streak counter keeps players coming back daily.

Word Duel

Two players race to build the longest word from a shared pool of 12 random letters. Each player types their best word in 90 seconds; longest valid word wins.

Constellation Maker

Click anywhere on a dark starfield canvas to place stars. Connect them to draw your own constellation. When you're done, press 'Name It' — the app uses the shape's geometry (centroid, arm count, density) to deterministically assign a poetic constellation name and a one-sentence myth. Download a shareable image of your named constellation.

Reflex Duel

Two players join via a room code. A countdown runs, then a colored circle flashes at a random screen position — first player to tap or click it wins the round. Best of 7 rounds. Reaction time is displayed after each round, and sub-200ms earns a 'Superhuman Reflex' badge. The winner shares a challenge card: 'I beat my friend 4-3 with a best reaction of 187ms — can you dethrone me?' Sessions end in under 3 minutes.

Gravity Doodle

Draw any shape on the canvas with your finger or mouse — a star, a spiral, your name — then press 'Drop' and watch hundreds of colorful sand particles fall from the top, pile up, and sculpt your drawing in particle form. Physics handled by Matter.js via CDN. A 'Save my sculpture' button exports the canvas as a shareable image. No win state — pure hypnotic emergent physics.

Reaction Diffusion Studio

A Gray-Scott reaction diffusion simulator running on HTML Canvas. Click or drag to seed chemical patterns anywhere on the canvas — watch Turing-style spots, stripes, and labyrinthine organism-like textures emerge in real time. Four preset modes (spots, coral, fingerprints, maze) plus three tunable sliders (feed rate, kill rate, diffusion ratio). A 'Save my organism' button exports the canvas as a full-resolution shareable image. No win state — pure mesmerizing emergent biology.

Boids Flock Painter

A canvas where a flock of 200 birds (boids) follows classic separation/alignment/cohesion rules. The user places colored 'seed' attractors by tapping or clicking — the flock swoops toward each one and leaves a glowing paint trail behind it. Each session produces a unique procedural painting shaped entirely by where the user taps. A 'Save my painting' button exports the canvas as a full-resolution image. No win state — pure hypnotic emergent art.

You Da Birthday

A birthday destiny calculator — enter your birthday and get your absurd cosmic role.

What MySpace Profile Were You?

A generator that takes your birth year, music taste, and a few personality prompts to produce a fake MySpace profile snapshot — complete with your 'Top 8' archetypes, profile song genre, About Me tone, and a named 2000s internet persona. Visual output designed to be screenshotted.
